Our Story

Two friends. One vision. Cambridge's most loved kebab van — built from scratch, served with pride, every single night.

Where it began

Two worlds,
one dream.

Hungry Kebab was born from a friendship between two people who grew up worlds apart. One came over from Turkey, carrying with him generations of authentic recipes, a love for real food, and a relentless passion for the grill. The other was born and raised in England, with the same hunger — not just for food, but to build something people would love.

They met through family friends and quickly discovered they shared the exact same dream — to bring genuine Turkish street food to Cambridge. Not a watered-down version. The real thing. Fresh ingredients, authentic flavours, generous portions, and the kind of service that makes people come back every week.

What started as an idea between friends became Cambridge's most talked-about kebab van. And they're just getting started.
